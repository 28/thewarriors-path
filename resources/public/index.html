Visualize the path Warriors took through the borrows of New York City

The map illustrates the Warriors' path and points of interest as depicted in the movie story. These are not the actual film locations, although they sometimes coincide. Some of the things are my free interpretation, like:

  • Path to and from the Lizzies' hideout.
  • The train from Union Square to Coney is the Q line.
  • The station next to the Central park is taken for The 96th Street station even though the Broadway one is depicted in the movie. This is because this is the only station that makes sense with regards to the modern train and metro system.
